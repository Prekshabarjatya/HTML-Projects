Chocolate Mousse

🍫 Calling All Chocoholics! 🍫 Indulge in this creamy, dreamy, light-as-air chocolate mousse — made in just 20 minutes with only 6 simple ingredients .

Ingredients

  • 200g (about 7 oz) dark chocolate, chopped or chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) heavy cream (or coconut cream for a lighter touch)
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) cold water

Instructions

  1. Melt the Chocolate & Sugar – In a heatproof bowl, combine chopped dark chocolate and sugar. Set the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler), stirring occasionally until smooth and melted. Remove from heat.
  2. Add the Cream & Flavorings – Stir in the heavy cream (or coconut cream), vanilla extract, and salt until fully combined and glossy.
  3. Whisk in Cold Water – Add the cold water to the chocolate mixture and whisk vigorously for about 30 seconds. This helps create that signature light-as-air texture!
  4. Chill Until Set – Divide the mousse into serving glasses or one large bowl. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set and slightly firm to the touch.
  5. Serve & Enjoy! – Top with whipped cream, fresh berries, or a dusting of cocoa powder. Dive in and enjoy every silky spoonful!
